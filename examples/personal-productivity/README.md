# Example: Personal Productivity

**Domain:** Individual life management  
**Use case:** A professional using AI to manage career transitions, health goals, and personal finance simultaneously

This example shows how an individual uses LC-OS governance to keep three life
pillars from bleeding into each other during a complex personal transition period:
job change + relocation + fitness commitment.

## What makes personal AI collaboration different

- Emotional stakes are higher — AI drift or bad advice has direct personal consequences
- Pillars can interfere (money stress bleeds into career decisions; health neglect
  affects work performance)
- Context is rich and personal — easy for AI to overstep or under-serve
- There is no team to catch mistakes — you are the only error-checker

LC-OS governs pillar separation, emotional boundaries, and numerical accuracy.
