## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

*This section is the single source of truth for who we're targeting.*

**Primary ICP:**
- Role: Operations Manager, Revenue Operations Lead, Head of Customer Success
- Company: B2B SaaS, 50–500 employees
- Pain: Manual intake processes taking >5 hours/week per team member
- Signal: Using Zendesk, HubSpot, or Salesforce (integration potential)
- Geography: US, Canada, UK

**Secondary ICP:**
- Founders/COOs at companies 10–50 employees
- Same pain, earlier buying stage

**Not our ICP (do not target):**
- Consumer apps
- Government / regulated industries (compliance friction too high for this cycle)
- Companies with <10 employees (too early for our pricing)

## 3. Core Messaging Principles

### Principle 1: Lead with time, not technology
Our audience cares about hours saved, not API architecture. Every piece of content
must lead with the problem (manual intake is killing your team's focus) before
mentioning the solution.

### Principle 2: Proof before claims
No claim without a number or customer quote. "Saves hours" is not acceptable.
"Saves 6.5 hours/week per ops team member (FlowDesk customer data, n=47)" is.

### Principle 3: One CTA per asset
Every piece of content has exactly one call to action. We do not ask readers to
"learn more, sign up for our webinar, and follow us." Pick one.

## 5. Campaign Policies

### Policy 1: Content approval
- All external content (blog, LinkedIn, email, ads) requires approval from Priya before publishing
- Turnaround SLA: 48 hours for review, 24 hours for changes
- AI can draft; Priya approves; no exceptions

### Policy 2: Numbers in content
- All numbers must be sourced from Canonical Numbers file before use
- If a number isn't in Canonical Numbers, it cannot be published
- AI must not invent or estimate statistics — only use what's pinned

### Policy 3: Campaign pause trigger
If any of the following occur, pause all paid spend and notify Priya immediately:
- CPL exceeds $180 for 3 consecutive days
- Any content published without approval
- Customer complaint about messaging accuracy
